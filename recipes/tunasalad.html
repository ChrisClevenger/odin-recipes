Tuna Salad

Description

When I get home from the gym or from a run, there are few things that fuel me up more than a healthy portion of tuna salad. Packed with protein, tuna salad helps replenish nutrients, restore muscles, and keep you feeling full.

One of the best things about tuna salad is how easy it is to make! Five minutes in the kitchen, and you're done--especially if you've pre-boiled your eggs!

Ingredients

  • 2 Boiled Eggs
  • 1 Packet of Tuna
  • 2 Tablespoon of Mayonnaise
  • 1 Tablespoon of Dill Relish
  • 1/2 Cup of Greens
  • Squirt of Sriracha
  • Pinch of Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Add tuna to bowl.
  2. Slice up eggs and add to bowl.
  3. Add mayonnaise, relish, and Sriracha to bowl.
  4. Slice or shred greens and add to bowl.
  5. Mix.
  6. Eat with a spoon or add to bread or lettuce wrap.
