Overnight Oats

Description

I like fucking easy recipes. It doesn't get any easier than overnight oats. Throw some oats, milk, and toppings in a container. Wait til morning. Viola! Overnight oats.

Ingredients

  • Rolled Oats
  • Oat Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Maple Syrup (Optional)

Steps

  1. Measure out 1/2 cup of oats and place into a tupperware container with lid.
  2. Top with oat milk. Don't worry about measuring. Just cover the oats. If you like them wet, add more. Stodgy? Add less.
  3. Drop in as much peanut butter as you think you can handle.
  4. Let the mixture sit in the fridge overnight. That's why they are called "overnight oats".
  5. Stir and enjoy!
